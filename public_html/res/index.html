EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Leseding Consulting Services a division of Leseding Computer Systems is a 100% black owned private company and an accredited Private FET Training Provider. It was registered as a close corporation in 2004 and it is level 1 BEE contributor with 135% procurement recognition.

Training and technical support programmes are geared towards training and development of employees in financial, technological and communication institutions.

MISSION STATEMENT

To become the best service provider that fulfill economic and development goals of the clients it serves.
