Further Education and Training Certificate: Technical Support NQF Level 4

We specialise in the quality delivery of lifelong learning and training interventions that suite clients’ needs

Qualification Name

Further Education and Training Certificate: Technical Support NQF Level 4

Total credits to be obtained after achievement: 197

Purpose of the Skills programme

The purpose of the qualification is to build a foundational entry into the field of Computer Sciences and Information Technology, specifically into the field of Systems support, covering knowledge needed for further study in the field of system support at Higher Education Levels

Possible Careers
  1. End User Support Technician
  2. IT Technician
  3. Sever Administrator
  4. Lan Administrator

Target Group
  1. learners wanting to specialize in the technical component of IT

Admission requirements
  1. Foundational skills in English and Mathematics at NQF Level 3 and Computer Literacy skills
  2. Grade 11 or equivalent.
